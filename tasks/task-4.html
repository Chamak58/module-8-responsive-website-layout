A plant-based diet is best

A plant-based diet is not only recommended to help reduce your cancer risk; it also has benefits during cancer treatment. Be sure to ask your doctor and dietitian if a plant-based diet is appropriate for your specific situation. Plant-based means that most of the foods you consume will come from plants. This includes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, beans, seeds and nuts.
